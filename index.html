About me

Artificial intelligence student, mathematics tutor, future artificial intelligence & machine learning engineer. Entry level Python developer, junior C++ practitioner, competent backend developer, entry level Data Scientist. Adept team player delivering results due tight deadlines.

Using Python and its libraries in everyday life, countless personal projects were produced. Having completed the Data Science module at the University, extraction of insightful information from big chunks of data, and presentations with graphs or charts is well established. In addition, manipulating data with Excel is a common practice.

For the past three years the creation of projects with C++ has yielded invaluable experience. Past work includes a desktop application that has a register & login system with a local MySQL database and a game called “Minesweeper”. Participant in many “Codingame” contests, where many prizes have been won versus other Lithuanian participants.

Working in a team of 6 individuals, solving backend problems in a collaborative environment to produce a final team project at the University of Manchester has been achieved. This project has taught to organize an effective workflow with the usage of Git and apply Trello for organizing tasks into boards. Furthermore, the pitching and presenting ideas to interested individuals has been significant.

Studying at a prestigious university has taught many lessons about how to properly approach a problem, implement and apply various algorithms, and maintain a proper code structure. Next year, it is expected to further delve into the art of data and acquire practical knowledge about state-of-the-art applications and development of artificial intelligence.
